Thanks for visiting.

I am an Assistant Professor of Radiology and Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Stony Brook University, New York. I also serve as the Director of Computational and Quantitative MRI Research in the Department of Radiology.

I lead the Autonomous MRI Systems (AMRIS) Research Lab, conducting research at the intersection of reinforcement learning, computational imaging, and quantitative MRI.
