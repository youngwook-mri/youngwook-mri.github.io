Research

The Autonomous MRI Systems Research Lab focuses on algorithmic advances in computational imaging and their clinical translation, including:

  • Intelligent imaging using reinforcement learning

  • Multi-dimensional image reconstruction algorithms with high-performance/parallel computing

  • Motion-tolerant body quantitative MRI

  • Novel non-Cartesian MRI data acquisition
