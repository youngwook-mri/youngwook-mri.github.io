Research

My research interests include almost all the core components in clinical MRI:

  • Pulse sequences

  • Model-based & data-driven image reconstruction

  • Rapid/motion-tolerant/non-Cartesian/dynamic/quantitative imaging

  • High performance computing

  • Magnetic susceptibility mapping

  • Liver fat/iron quantification

  • Inverse problems & numerical optimization

  • Statistical signal/image processing & analysis
